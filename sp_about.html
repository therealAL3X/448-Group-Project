Our Story

We all have trouble in certain classes, either it's a difficult subject, or we are just having a hard time grasping the material itself. But we all have a common goal, pass our class by showing understanding of the material. Doing this requires a lot of studying, and research shows that studying increases test scores and we all know this. Research also shows that distractions while studying significantly causes a decrease in test scores. What is the best way to minimize distractions and increase your test scores?

We found that it is through Study Groups, that is why we decided to build "Retrievers Together" which is a study group application which pairs you with other students willing and motivated to raise their test scores by studying in an efficient manner. Our system allows you to take a short quiz that asks your studying preferences and habits, then pairs you with other students in the same class who have similar study habits. Our system offers study habit trackers, study tips, and also reviews for other students that allow you to give feedback to the student as well as use to ensure that students aren't just freeloading off of your study time. All this is paired into an intuitive design which makes it easier than ever to find a study group on campus and pass that test!learning needs.
